Top Skills to Earn Money Online

1. Freelancing
Freelancing platforms like Fiverr, Upwork, and Freelancer allow you to offer skills like design, writing, and coding to global clients.
2. Graphic Design
Use tools like Canva, Photoshop, and Illustrator to create logos, social media posts, banners, etc., and sell your design services.
3. Web Development
Learn HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks to build websites for clients or your own online business.
4. Content Writing
Write articles, blogs, or scripts for YouTube channels, websites, or clients and get paid per word or article.
5. Digital Marketing
Learn SEO, social media marketing, and email campaigns to help businesses grow online.
